# Write up

If you know me and hang out in the social media circles that i hang out at, you start to notice this pattern.
 I write a lot. I write about code, about engineering, about philosophy, about life, and about the things that interest me. 
 I write about the things that I think are important, and I write about the things that I think are interesting. 
 I write because I have an itch to express myself, and I write because I have an itch to share my thoughts with the world. The ones new to this, 
 you might have only seen me sharing memes and pieces i found. I used to write or share something original every day.

### What do I share? or why?

Its a funny situation. I have had many complain i post a lot, told me they have muted me. Still, it has never stopped me from posting unrelated things, 
and I have never stopped sharing my thoughts. This takes me to the few questions only my favorite people asked or they know the answer to.
 For someone who had to walk and tried to charter a dominion of dark that we call life, I know how it is, to be in a path where no one truly understands your journey.
Everyone has their own path, everyone has their own struggles, everyone deals with the same things different ways. Thats the base of this.
But mostly its a common fact that most think their struggle or their niche is their own to bear and potentially there is no one else who understands this.
